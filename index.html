❧ The Table

Good food, no fuss.

Lunch here is usually a big bowl of salad piled high with whatever's fresh, a board of pâté with toast and a good chutney, or something sweet if you've saved room. Nothing complicated — just done properly, and in portions that don't leave you hungry.

The room's nothing to write home about, if we're honest, but it's warm and easy, and nobody's going to rush you out if you're still finishing your coffee an hour later.

Regulars still call us the secret conker — a nod to the old horse chestnut by the back door that half the town used as a landmark long before we ever put up a sign.
